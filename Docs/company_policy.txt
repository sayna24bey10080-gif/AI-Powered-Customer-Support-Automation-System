ABC Technologies – Company Policies and Refund Guidelines (2025)

REFUND POLICY
=============

Eligibility:
- Refund requests must be submitted within 30 days of the charge date.
- Refunds are available if: (a) a billing error occurred, (b) the service was unavailable for more than 72 consecutive hours due to our fault, or (c) the customer cancels within 7 days of initial sign-up (no-questions-asked).
- After 30 days, refunds are granted at management discretion only.

Process:
- Submit a refund request via the billing portal or email billing@abctech.com.
- Include: account email, invoice number, reason for request.
- Refund requests are reviewed within 3–5 business days.
- Approved refunds are processed to the original payment method within 7–10 business days.
- Refund requests exceeding $500 require supervisor approval before processing.

Non-Refundable Items:
- One-time setup fees and onboarding session fees are non-refundable.
- Add-on purchases (storage, extra users) are non-refundable once the billing period has started.
- Annual plans cancelled after 30 days receive a prorated credit for unused months (not a cash refund), unless a billing error occurred.

SUBSCRIPTION CANCELLATION POLICY
==================================
- Customers may cancel at any time through Account Settings → Subscription → Cancel Plan.
- Upon cancellation: access continues until end of the current paid period. No further charges are made.
- Data retention after cancellation: your data is retained for 60 days post-cancellation. After that, it is permanently deleted.
- To cancel and immediately delete data, contact support@abctech.com.
- Reactivation: cancelled accounts can be reactivated within 60 days with all data intact.
- Subscription cancellation requests flagged as high-value (Enterprise or accounts >$1000 in lifetime value) are routed to a customer success manager for a retention conversation before finalizing.

ACCOUNT CLOSURE POLICY
=======================
- Account closure is permanent and different from cancellation. All data is immediately queued for deletion.
- To close an account: submit a written request to support@abctech.com from the registered email address.
- Closure requests are processed within 2 business days.
- Account closure requires supervisor approval if there are outstanding invoices or active disputes.
- GDPR/CCPA data deletion requests are handled separately; submit to privacy@abctech.com.

COMPENSATION POLICY
===================
- Service credits are issued automatically when uptime falls below our SLA (99.9% monthly uptime guarantee).
- Credit calculation: 10x the hourly cost for each hour of downtime beyond the SLA threshold.
- Credits appear on the next invoice and cannot be converted to cash.
- Compensation requests for incidents not covered by the automatic credit system require escalation to the support manager.

DISPUTE RESOLUTION
==================
- Billing disputes must be raised within 60 days of the charge.
- First point of contact: billing@abctech.com.
- Unresolved disputes escalate to: support-manager@abctech.com.
- ABC Technologies aims to resolve all disputes within 10 business days.

PRIVACY AND DATA POLICY
========================
- We do not sell customer data to third parties.
- Data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3).
- Employees access customer data only for support purposes and with the customer's consent.
- Full privacy policy: https://www.abctech.com/privacy

For policy questions, contact: policy@abctech.com
