ABC Technologies – Pricing Guide (2025)

SUBSCRIPTION PLANS
==================

Basic Plan – $10/month
- Up to 5 users
- 10 GB cloud storage
- Email support (response within 48 hours)
- Access to core features: task management, reporting dashboard, file sharing
- Mobile app access (iOS and Android)
- No credit card required for 14-day free trial

Pro Plan – $25/month
- Up to 25 users
- 100 GB cloud storage
- Priority email + live chat support (response within 4 hours)
- All Basic features plus: advanced analytics, API access, custom integrations, automated workflows
- Single Sign-On (SSO) support
- Role-based access control
- Audit logs (90-day retention)
- 30-day free trial available

Enterprise Plan – Custom Pricing (Contact Sales)
- Unlimited users
- Unlimited cloud storage
- 24/7 dedicated account manager and phone support
- All Pro features plus: custom SLA, on-premise deployment option, white-label branding
- Advanced security: SOC2 Type II compliance, HIPAA compliance, custom data retention policies
- Priority onboarding and training sessions
- Volume discounts available for annual billing

BILLING OPTIONS
===============
- Monthly billing: pay each month, cancel anytime
- Annual billing: save 20% compared to monthly pricing
  - Basic Annual: $96/year (save $24)
  - Pro Annual: $240/year (save $60)
  - Enterprise: negotiated annually

ADD-ONS (available for Basic and Pro plans)
===========================================
- Extra Storage: $5/month per additional 50 GB
- Additional Users (Basic): $2/user/month beyond the 5-user limit
- Advanced Security Package: $10/month (includes SSO, audit logs for Basic)
- Dedicated Onboarding Session: $199 one-time fee

PAYMENT METHODS
===============
- Credit/Debit Card (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- PayPal
- Bank Transfer (Enterprise only)
- Purchase Orders accepted for Enterprise annual contracts

UPGRADES AND DOWNGRADES
========================
- You can upgrade your plan at any time; charges are prorated immediately.
- Downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing cycle.
- Switching from monthly to annual billing: discount applies from next cycle.

TAXES
=====
- Prices shown are exclusive of applicable taxes (GST, VAT, etc.)
- Tax is calculated based on billing address at checkout.
